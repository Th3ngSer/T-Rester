About Us

At T-Rester, we believe in serving the best food with a smile. Our chefs use only the freshest ingredients to create mouth-watering dishes that will leave you coming back for more.

Whether you're here for a quick bite or a leisurely meal, our friendly staff is dedicated to providing you with an unforgettable dining experience.

Featured Dishes

Discover our signature dishes that showcase the rich flavors of Cambodian cuisine.

Amok Trei (Fish Amok)

Traditional Khmer steamed fish in coconut curry, a true Cambodian classic.

Lok Lak (Stir-fried Beef)

Tender stir-fried beef served with fresh lettuce, tomatoes, and a flavorful lime and pepper sauce.

Khmer Curry (Red Curry)

A rich and aromatic coconut-based curry with your choice of chicken or vegetables, subtly spiced.
